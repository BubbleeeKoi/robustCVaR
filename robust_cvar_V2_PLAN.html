三、核心数学模型

3.1 Portfolio Loss

$$ L_{t+1} = -w_t^T r_{t+1} + c||w_t - w_{t-1}||_1 $$ ---

3.2 RCVaR

$$ RCVaR = \max_q \sum q_i L_i $$ $$ 0 \le q_i \le \frac{\kappa_i}{\alpha B} $$ ---

3.3 Risk Function (核心创新)

$$ \kappa_\theta(s) = 1 + \kappa_{max} \cdot \sigma(f_\theta(s)) $$ ---
